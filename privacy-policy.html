Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how TeslaLink ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our web-based application (the "Service"). Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

TeslaLink is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name, email address, or any other contact information.
  • Your location data.
  • Your IP address or device identifiers.
  • Any information about your vehicle.

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. No user accounts are required to use the Service, and we have no way of identifying individual users.

2. How Your Data is Stored

All data related to your use of TeslaLink, such as your customized list of applications, selected region, and other settings, is stored exclusively in your web browser's Local Storage on your device (i.e., in your Tesla's computer).

  • This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • We have no access to this data.
  • If you clear your browser's data, your TeslaLink configuration will be permanently deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

TeslaLink allows you to add and launch third-party websites and web applications. When you use these third-party services, you are subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites you access through our Service.

4. QR Code Functionality

The "Scan QR" feature uses a temporary, secure connection via Firebase to transmit a URL from your phone to your Tesla's browser. This session is anonymous, encrypted, and ephemeral. We do not log or store the URLs or any data associated with this process.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
