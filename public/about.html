Fully Customizable

With large, touch-friendly buttons and a clean layout, TeslaLink is designed for easy use on the Tesla touchscreen. Light and dark themes automatically match your system preference.

Get quick access to popular streaming and utility apps for your specific region. Switch regions at any time to explore what's popular worldwide.

Private & Secure

TeslaLink is completely free and respects your privacy. All your app configurations are stored locally in your browser, not on our servers.
