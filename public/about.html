What is TeslaLink?

TeslaLink is a free, web-based platform designed to enhance your Tesla's browser experience. It provides a clean, organized, and visually appealing dashboard to launch web apps, entertainment, and utilities. With features like Live Channels, QR code scanning, and fullscreen mode, TeslaLink transforms your Tesla's screen into a powerful entertainment and productivity hub.
